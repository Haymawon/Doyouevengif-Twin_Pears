About Us

Hello, I'm Lain!

This website, Twin Pears, is completely just for fun! It's just me and my friend Aaliyah sharing our most random, childish thoughts.

One midnight—April 12, 2025—Aaliyah and I were having a dumb vs. dumb conversation (don't ask 🙃), and we randomly decided to make a website. And here it is!

What started as just the two of us has grown into a little team of five. Somehow, it works—we're all different kinds of weird, and it blends perfectly.

What did ChatGPT do?

ChatGPT helped us write stuff, fix grammar, and gave us ideas when we were stuck. Basically our unpaid intern 😌
